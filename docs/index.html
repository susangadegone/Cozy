Daily routine

Starts with how you're feeling today.

Tell Cozy if you're tired, so-so, or good to go — and it adjusts how many chores to show. Tap any chore to see its full detail, bump it a day, or skip a week.

  • Energy-aware daily cap so you never feel overwhelmed
  • Inline check-off with confetti when you finish
  • Bump, skip, or reschedule any chore from the detail card
Getting started

Ready in two minutes, not two hours.

Cozy seeds a starter schedule from your rooms and pace. Browse the chore library to add more anytime — everything comes with a sensible default frequency already set.

  • Onboarding that builds your first schedule for you
  • Curated chore library organized by room
  • Add from the library or type your own in seconds
Progress & streaks

Small wins that actually feel like wins.

An animated streak ring fills toward each milestone — 5, 7, 14, 30 days. Earn badges for real achievements. See everything laid out in a week or month calendar.

  • Streak ring with color that deepens as momentum builds
  • 7 badges for milestones that mean something
  • Week and month calendar with dot indicators per day
