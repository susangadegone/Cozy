Features

Designed for real life,
not perfect homes.

Energy-aware scheduling

Tell Cozy how you're feeling each morning — tired, so-so, or ready to go — and it adjusts how many chores to show you.

Streaks with milestones

Build momentum with an animated streak ring that fills toward your next milestone: 5, 7, 14, and 30 days.

Room-by-room clarity

Chores are grouped by room with color-coded cards, so you always know exactly what needs doing and where.

Flexible rescheduling

Bump a chore by a day, skip a week, or pick any date — right from the chore detail card, without losing your rhythm.

Badges that mean something

Earn badges for real milestones — first chore, perfect week, all rooms done. Each one is a genuine record of your progress.

A chore library to start from

Browse a curated library of common chores with default frequencies already set. Add one in seconds, or type your own.
